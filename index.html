Project Idea

Follow the crumbs

On every app store there are millions of different apps that provide users with thousands of unique abilities. There are apps for games, dating, bookings, photos, social media, and critiquing restaurants. I found that apps that rate restaurants and food sparked my interest for some reason, this gave me the idea to have my project idea around this. After much research into these types of apps I found that there is one for nearly every food we consume daily and where to buy it from, every food apart from bread! It may seem simple however there is no app for the available for people who want to know where to buy the best bread!

Motivation

The motivation behind this idea was that I tried to think of what people consume nearly every day, first thing I thought was coffee, unfortunately there is already an app for that, then I thought about bread as it’s something I eat a lot, and funny enough there was no app that rates bread! This app would be very similar to a few other apps as really at its core it’s a review app like many others, however this one is specifically targeted at rating and reviewing bread. I know many people who love bread even more than me so I feel the idea is one that would be one that others would use often.

Description

“Follow the crumbs” is the name I came up with for this app and it would work based on the user’s reviews, when the app selected it will open to a screen that gives the users the option to selected where they are and what type of bread they desire. After they enter in their location, the user is then greeted with a map that displays the best breads and where to find them within their searched vicinity. The bakery displayed would be rated based on each type of bread and their overall rating, for example, if a certain bakery does terrific sourdough (10 out of 10), but it bakes an awful croissant (1 out of 10) then the overall store rating would average out and would most likely not be at the top of the listings. If the user however searches for just sourdough, this same bakery will be displayed as one of the top local place to buy from. The app also allows users to add multiple filters so that their search can be tailormade to what they are looking for. These filters include a “sort by” filter that allows for the user to search for bread in range of cost, rating and even popularity.
For places to be added to “Follow the Crumbs” each bakery would have to set up an account with their items displayed via the app, this allows for easy reviews and rating of each item from the users. Bakeries would want to add themselves on to the site as it allows for greater publicity and it is absolutely free, if they chose not to add themselves then they would be putting their business at a loss as users are more likely to go to places that are reviewed on “follow the crumbs”. This app provides great potential for small local bakeries that only a small group of people may know about, these are the places of business that would improve the most as buyers will be provided to opportunities that they before were not aware of.
“Follow the Crumbs” could also be used in conjunction with food delivery apps, this would be done if the user wants bread delivered to them they can easily search on our app what the reviews are for bread, this will then incline the user to buy from a company that is recommended on “Follow the Crumbs”.
This app implements ideas from other incredibly successful apps such as Zomato which is the top rated and download food review app in the world. Zomato has over twenty-two thousand reviews with an average rating of 4.6 out of 5, there is clearly a demand for apps that are like this and “Follow the crumbs” fills a void that is simple yet valuable. This app could become a staple on every smart phone.

Tools and Technologies

Follow the crumbs would be available on all smart phones, therefore a user would need a smartphone to access the app, possibly in the future the app could also expand to becoming available on computers. The software needed to create this app would need to work on both android and iOS, this does bring up a few difficulties as the two operating systems use different programming languages. iOS programming language is Swift 4 or Objective-C where Android uses Java or Kotlin, I would suggest we begin on one app and then transition to the next, from what I’ve found is that doing this is the best approach as it allows your app to at least be available on one platform and gain traction. I have also found its apparently easier to accomplish then try to do both at once!

Skills Required

Researching on how apps that are similar to “Follow the crumbs” I found that it is not that easy, and a few skills are needed to make this app. Firstly, to even begin the developers would have to have knowledge with a programming language, be familiar to mobile programming interfaces, know how to implement the use of cross platforming interfaces and know how to implement security on the app. These skills are not learnt overnight and to develop this app would take time. It is feasible to learn these skills, however it would take time. We would also need a great marketing strategy, there are millions of app therefore it is not easy to stand out. We would need to advertise extensively so users can see what the app provides, nobody will download it unless they know about it!

Outcome

Once “follow the crumbs” is realised I honestly believe that it would fill a missing aspect in the app community, this app even though it wouldn’t change the world it would add a great tool for business and consumers. I can see people from all around the world using this app as bread is a product people consume every day, it doesn’t matter if the user is in Australia, Japan, USA or Europe, the app is just as relevant anywhere around the world. As previous mentioned I believe this app would become a staple of most people’s phone, it’s a benefit for every single person that would use it and its honestly built so small business have a chance to shine when they otherwise would be forgotten about.
